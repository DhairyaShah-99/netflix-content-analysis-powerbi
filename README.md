# Netflix Content Analysis (Power BI)

This project analyzes the **Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset** using Power BI to uncover trends in content distribution, growth over time, genre popularity, and global availability.

The dashboard provides an interactive view of Netflix's catalog to help understand how the platform's content library has evolved and how different factors such as **genre, country, and release year** influence the distribution of titles.

## Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard enables exploration of Netflix content through interactive filters and multiple analytical visuals.

Users can filter the analysis by:

- Content Type (Movie / TV Show)
- Rating
- Country
- Release Year

## Key Business Insights

### Content Distribution
- Netflix catalog consists of **~19K total titles**
- **13K+ Movies** and **6K+ TV Shows**
- Movies represent roughly **68% of the platform's content**

### Content Growth Over Time
- Significant growth in titles added after **2015**
- Rapid expansion of Netflix's catalog between **2017–2020**

### Genre Analysis
- **International Movies** and **Dramas** are among the most common genres
- Comedies and Action content also represent a significant share of titles

### Global Distribution
- Netflix content is distributed globally across multiple regions
- Certain countries contribute a higher number of titles to the platform

### Content Trends
- The dashboard highlights how Netflix expanded its content library over time
- Shows the relationship between **release years and catalog expansion**

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

- **Movies vs TV Shows Distribution**
- **Netflix Content Growth Over Time**
- **Top 10 Genres by Number of Titles**
- **Global Map of Netflix Content by Country**
- **KPI Cards for Total Titles, Movies, TV Shows, Movie Share, and Genre Count**
- **Interactive Filters for deeper exploration**
